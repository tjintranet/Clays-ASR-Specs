ASR: Processing Rules

Standard rules and specifications for ASR processing

General Rules

Paperback Only
  • All trim sizes allowed
  • No separate illustration sections
Restrictions
  • No foiling / embossing / spot UV
  • No 5th colour
  • No uncoated cover board
Cover Board Standards
  • 220gsm cover board as standard
  • Print on the reverse of 220gsm stock allowed
