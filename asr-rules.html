Production Specifications

Inside Printing

Inside printing is allowed.

Cover Board
  • 220gsm Incada Silk standard stock
  • ASRs do not print on the uncoated side of the board
Cover Prices
  • UNPRICED* with the exception of Hachette Children's titles
  • Automation only set to accept priced ASRs for Hachette Children's
  • Historically one series of JM titles contain prices
